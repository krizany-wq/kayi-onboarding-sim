Simulation guide

  • Onboarding tab: Select Individual or Business, then step through all screens. Use Simulate error buttons to test retry and escalation flows.
  • Upgrade tab: Starts from a Tier 1 home screen. Tap Transfer to hit the limit, or tap the upgrade banner. Choose Individual or Business upgrade path.
  • Section 12 fields: NEW OTP phone verification, place of birth, occupation, source of funds, expected usage, PEP declaration, and risk classification are all active in both simulators.
  • Risk engine: Select "Gift or inheritance" as source of funds, or answer Yes to any PEP question — the risk classification will change and MLRO hold path will activate.
  • Error paths: Try these Simulate BVN mismatch 3× → escalation. Simulate face match fail 3× → escalation. Simulate expired document → retry prompt.
